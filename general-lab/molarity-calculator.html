How to use this calculator

Select what you want to solve for, fill in the known values, and the result updates instantly. Molarity (M) is defined as moles of solute per liter of solution.

When solving for molarity or mass, you need to provide the molecular weight (MW) of your compound. When solving for moles, MW is not required.
