How to use this calculator

Enter the volume and concentration you need, then search for a substance. The calculator will tell you exactly how much to weigh out (for solids) or measure (for liquids), plus step-by-step preparation instructions.

For pH buffers like Tris-HCl and phosphate buffers, the calculator includes notes on which acid or base to use for pH adjustment and the effective buffering range.

About molecular-weight-based calculations

For molar concentrations (M, mM, µM, nM), the calculator uses: mass = M × V × MW, where M is molarity in mol/L, V is volume in liters, and MW is the molecular weight in g/mol. For mass-based concentrations (mg/mL, g/L, %(w/v)), no molecular weight is needed — the calculation is direct.
